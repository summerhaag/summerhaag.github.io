---
title: "Computational study of non-unitary partitions"
authors: "A.P. Akande, Tyler Genao, Summer Haag, Maurice Hendon, Neelima Pulagam, Robert Schnider, Andrew V. Sills"
abstract: >
  Following Cayley, MacMahon, and Sylvester, we define a non-unitary partition as
  an integer partition with no part equal to one, denoted ν(n). Building on prior
  work, we refine and prove earlier conjectures about the growth of ν(n). Key results
  include proving that p(n) ~ ν(n)√(n/ζ(2)) as n approaches infinity, and
  establishing Ramanujan-like congruences such as p(5n) ≡ ν(5n) (mod 5).
date: 2021-12-01
arxiv: "https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.03264"
